About



"A small web app that serves medical questions"

Website is made with Flask as backend with Jinja2 and Flask-Bootsrap to render frontend. JQuery and AJAX for form submissions to refresh multiple choice questions without reloading the page. Database used started with SQLite3 then migrated to PostgreSQL for easier hosting to Heroku.

Other extensions used were: FlaskSQLAlchemy (database operations), Flask-Nav (navbar rendering), Flask-WTForms (form rendering and backend logic), Fkask-SSLify (security).

Adding questions whenever I can.
